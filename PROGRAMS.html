Academic Programs

Programs Offered

BSCRIM - Bachelor of Science in Criminology

The Bachelor of Science in Criminology is a four-year degree program generally offered to students who are interested to pursue a career in law enforcement, correctional administration, security, and crime prevention. Students in this program will be able to learn different practices and theories of laws related to criminal behavior which leads to effective law enforcement.A graduate of this program is required to take and pass the Criminologist Licensure Examination to be considered a Criminologist.

BSIT - Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology is a computer-related program that prepares students on how to install, operate, develop, maintain, and administer computer applications. It also equips students with the basic ability to conceptualize, design, and implement software applications.This program is for students who are looking to have a career as an Applications Developer, Web Developer, Network Engineer, Web Administrator, Database Administrator, etc.

BSA - Bachelor of Science in Accountancy

The Bachelor of Science in Accountancy is composed of subjects in financial, public, and managerial accounting, as well as audit, administration, business laws, and taxation. This program is more than just business subjects. Students will also take subjects about banking and finance, government, social services, and education. Information technology concepts are also integrated to create a more systematic and organized way of storing business data.This undergraduate program is suitable for students who are planning to take the licensure examinations and become a Certified Public Accountant (CPA).

BSBA - Bachelor of Science in Business Administration

The Bachelor of Science in Business Administration program is designed to provide students with familiarity in business operations. Students in this program will develop their critical decision-making skills for strategic and executive work which are required for competing in the global business world.This program can be taken with various specializations in management, marketing management, financial management, management accounting, business economics, etc. Also, this is suitable for students who have, or want to have the leadership skills necessary to have successful careers in managerial and supervisory positions.

BEE & BSE - Bachelor of Elementary Education

The Bachelor of Elementary Education is a four-year degree program that combines theory and practice designed to prepare students for teaching in primary schools.

The Bachelor of Secondary Education is a four-year degree program that combines theory and practice designed to prepare students for teaching in secondary schools.
